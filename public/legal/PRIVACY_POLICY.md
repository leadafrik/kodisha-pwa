## 8. YOUR RIGHTS AS A DATA SUBJECT

Under the Data Protection Act, 2019, you have the following rights:

### 8.1 Right to Access (Art. 38)
You may request:
- Confirmation of whether we process your personal data
- Access to your personal data
- Information about processing purposes and recipients
- A copy of your data (free of charge for first request)

**How to Exercise:** Email info@leadafrik.com or use in-app "Download My Data" feature.

### 8.2 Right to Rectification (Art. 39)
You may request correction of:
- Inaccurate personal data
- Incomplete personal data

**How to Exercise:** Update your profile settings or contact info@leadafrik.com.

### 8.3 Right to Erasure (Art. 40) ("Right to be Forgotten")
You may request deletion of your personal data when:
- Data is no longer necessary for original purpose
- You withdraw consent (where processing is based on consent)
- You object to processing and no overriding legitimate grounds exist
- Data was unlawfully processed
- Legal obligations require erasure

**Limitations:** We may retain data for legal obligations, dispute resolution, or fraud prevention.

**How to Exercise:** Use in-app "Delete Account" or email info@leadafrik.com.

### 8.4 Right to Restrict Processing (Art. 41)
You may request restriction when:
- You contest the accuracy of data
- Processing is unlawful but you prefer restriction over erasure
- We no longer need the data but you need it for legal claims
- You've objected to processing (pending verification)

### 8.5 Right to Data Portability (Art. 42)
You may request:
- Receiving your data in a structured, commonly used format (JSON, CSV)
- Transmitting your data to another controller

**How to Exercise:** Email info@leadafrik.com with portability request.

### 8.6 Right to Object (Art. 43)
You may object to:
- Processing based on legitimate interests
- Direct marketing (at any time)
- Profiling for marketing purposes

**How to Exercise:** Click "Unsubscribe" in emails or email info@leadafrik.com.

### 8.7 Right Not to be Subject to Automated Decision-Making (Art. 44)
You have the right not to be subject to decisions based solely on automated processing (including profiling) that produce legal or similarly significant effects.

**Our Use:** We use algorithms for search ranking and recommendations but not for significant automated decisions affecting you.
