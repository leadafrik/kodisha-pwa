# PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: March 1, 2026

Agrisoko is committed to handling personal data with care and in line with Kenya's Data Protection Act, 2019. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices available to you when you use agrisoko254.com and related Agrisoko services.

Agrisoko is the marketplace brand operated by LeadAfrik Agricultural Solutions.

## 1. Who we are

Agrisoko is a Kenyan agricultural marketplace that helps farmers, buyers, agrovets, and agricultural service providers connect directly. The platform is operated by LeadAfrik Agricultural Solutions.

Contact for privacy questions or requests:
- Email: info@leadafrik.com
- WhatsApp / Phone: +254 796 389 192
- Website: https://www.agrisoko254.com

You may also raise a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) in Kenya if you believe your data rights have been infringed.

## 2. Data we collect

We may collect:
- Account data: full name, email address, phone number, password, referral code, and account role.
- Profile data: profile photo, county, constituency, ward, and other profile fields you choose to add.
- Marketplace data: listings, buy requests, messages, ratings, reports, favorites, and moderation records.
- Verification data: if you choose to verify, we collect your ID images and selfie for identity review.
- Technical data: IP address, browser, device information, basic logs, and app diagnostics.
- Consent data: records of your acceptance of our Terms, Privacy Policy, and data processing consent.

## 3. Verification data and ID images

Identity verification on Agrisoko is optional. It is used to increase trust, improve visibility, and support moderation.

If you choose to upload ID verification documents:
- we use them only for identity review and trust checks;
- access is limited to authorized review and support workflows;
- we delete the uploaded ID images promptly after the admin review is completed, whether the review is approved or rejected; and
- we retain only the verification outcome, timestamps, and any review notes needed for trust, fraud prevention, audit, or dispute handling.

Please do not upload documents unless you are ready to proceed with verification.

## 4. Why we use your data

We use personal data to:
- create and secure your account;
- publish listings and buy requests;
- enable messaging, moderation, reviews, and platform safety;
- verify identity where you choose to verify;
- send transactional emails, alerts, and important service notices;
- improve performance, debug issues, and monitor abuse; and
- comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

We only use marketing messages where you have consented or where the law otherwise allows.

## 5. What is public on the platform

Because Agrisoko is a marketplace, some information is visible to other users in context. This can include:
- your display name or business name;
- your verification status;
- listing details, buy request details, photos, pricing, and location information you choose to post; and
- contact details you choose to include in marketplace activity.

Please post only information you are comfortable sharing for trade purposes.

## 6. Sharing with service providers

We may use third-party providers to run the platform, including hosting, storage, authentication, messaging, analytics, and support tools. These providers may process data on our behalf only for service delivery, security, and operations.

Examples may include cloud hosting, media storage, email providers, error monitoring, and social sign-in providers.

We may also disclose data where required by law, regulation, court order, or to protect users, Agrisoko, or the public.

## 7. Cookies and analytics

Agrisoko uses essential cookies and local storage to keep you signed in, remember preferences, and secure the app.

Where non-essential analytics or advertising tags are used, we aim to rely on consent before activating them.

## 8. Retention

We keep personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purpose it was collected, including platform operation, dispute handling, security, and legal obligations.

In particular:
- account and marketplace records may be retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period after closure;
- moderation, report, and fraud-prevention records may be retained where necessary for safety and compliance; and
- ID verification images are deleted promptly after review, while the verification decision and review metadata may be retained.

## 9. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request:
- access to your personal data;
- correction of inaccurate data;
- deletion of data that no longer needs to be retained;
- objection to certain processing; or
- restriction of processing in appropriate cases.

To make a request, contact info@leadafrik.com.

## 10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data, but no system can guarantee absolute security. You are also responsible for protecting your password and using the platform carefully.

## 11. Children

Agrisoko is not intended for children under 18. Do not create an account or submit personal data if you are under 18.

## 12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version published on Agrisoko will apply from the date shown above.
